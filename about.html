Welcome to The Trek

The Trek is a fitness and activity tracking app designed to motivate and connect people through movement. It helps you track your physical activity—like steps, distance, and time—using data from Google Fit and other supported sources.

Our Vision

We aim to build a healthy and active community by turning everyday movement into fun, progress, and friendly competition. Whether you’re walking, running, or exploring new places, The Trek helps you stay motivated and accountable.

Features

  • Track steps, distance, and calories burned
  • Connect seamlessly with Google Fit
  • View your daily and weekly progress
  • Participate in challenges and community rankings
