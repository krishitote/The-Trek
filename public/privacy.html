Privacy Policy

Last updated: October 28, 2025

The Trek (“we”, “our”, “us”) operates the fitness tracking app “The Trek”. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect information when you use our app and services, including data accessed from Google Fit.

1. Data Accessed

With your explicit consent, The Trek may access the following data types from your Google Fit account:

  • Step count (number of steps taken)
  • Distance walked or run
  • Calories burned
  • Activity duration and type (e.g., walking, running)

We also collect basic user information you provide when registering (such as your name, email address, and profile photo).

2. Data Usage

We use your data solely for the following purposes:

  • To display accurate fitness insights and progress statistics within the app.
  • To generate personalized leaderboards and activity rankings.
  • To maintain your account and provide customer support.
  • To analyze aggregated, anonymized data for improving app performance and features.

We do not use your data for advertising or any purpose unrelated to the app’s functionality.

3. Data Sharing

The Trek does not sell, rent, or trade your personal or Google Fit data. Limited data may be shared only with trusted service providers that help us operate the app, such as hosting and database providers, and only as necessary to perform those services.

Data is never shared with third parties for marketing or analytics unrelated to The Trek.

4. Data Storage & Protection

User data (such as email, profile information, and activity summaries) is securely stored in a PostgreSQL database hosted by Render. Data transmitted between The Trek and Google Fit is encrypted using HTTPS and OAuth 2.0 protocols.

We implement strict technical and organizational safeguards to prevent unauthorized access, loss, or alteration of your information.

5. Data Retention & Deletion

We retain user account data only as long as your account remains active. You can delete your account or request deletion of all stored data by contacting us at thetrekkenya@gmail.com.

Upon deletion, all associated personal and fitness data are permanently removed from our servers within 7 business days.

6. Data Access Revocation

You can revoke The Trek’s access to your Google Fit data at any time by visiting your Google Account permissions page: https://myaccount.google.com/permissions.

7. Children’s Privacy

The Trek is not intended for users under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children. If you believe a child has provided us personal data, please contact us to remove it.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Updates will be posted on this page with a revised “last updated” date.

9. Contact Us

For any questions, requests, or concerns about this Privacy Policy, contact us at:

Email: thetrekkenya@gmail.com
